Memet exists because everyone deserves to feel heard — regardless of time, place, or what they're going through.

The Mission

To make emotional support genuinely accessible. Not as a therapy replacement — but as a compassionate presence for the 3am thoughts, the overwhelming days, the feelings you can't quite explain to anyone else.

The Approach

Validation first. Always. Before advice, before solutions — feelings deserve to be fully acknowledged. Every response is crafted to make you feel held, not just heard.

Aditya Pandey

Founder & Developer · 2nd Year Engineering

Built Memet because he believed AI could be warm. That technology didn't have to feel cold — it could feel like a friend who's genuinely always there. As a student navigating pressure and uncertainty, he understood the need for a judgment-free space.
